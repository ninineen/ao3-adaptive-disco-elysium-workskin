Author's note

This isn't meant to be a perfect recreation of Disco Elysium's UI. I just wanted to bring some of the skill colours into AO3 without making people switch themes or read on a giant black background.

I also wanted it to work nicely on phones, so there aren't any fixed-width containers or layout tricks that break on smaller screens. It should look decent whether you're reading on desktop, mobile, light mode, dark mode, or whatever cursed setup you've assembled for yourself.

The goal was simple: keep the disco flavour, keep it readable.
