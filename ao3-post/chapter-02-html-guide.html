Copy this into your chapter body while the work skin is active. Switch to HTML mode in the AO3 editor before pasting.

Each element is shown in context. Mix and match — you don't have to use all of them.

Alert Banners

Two span tags inside the p: the first is the label, the second is the value. The skin stacks them automatically.

Thought Gained The Cop of the Apocalypse

Health Damaged -1

Morale Healed +1

HTML 
<p class="de-alert thought">
  <span>Thought Gained</span>
  <span>The Cop of the Apocalypse</span>
</p>

<p class="de-alert health-down">
  <span>Health Damaged</span>
  <span>-1</span>
</p>

<p class="de-alert morale-up">
  <span>Morale Healed</span>
  <span>+1</span>
</p>

Dialogue Lines

The de-convo class handles the hanging indent. The speaker or skill label goes in its own span at the start.

KIM KITSURAGI — "Detective, I highly advise against drinking from a thermos you found inside a rusted storm drain."

ELECTROCHEMISTRY [Medium: Success] — But look at it! The strange, iridescent film resting on top of the liquid promises a kaleidoscope of illicit thrills. Just a little sip. It will make you feel like a god.

VOLITION [Easy: Failure] — The lieutenant is right. It's a blatant biohazard. But you are so, so weak against the call of the void.

HALF LIGHT — FIRE. PURE ALKALINE FIRE IN YOUR VEINS. YOUR ORGANS ARE MELTING.

HTML 
<!-- Named character speaking -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-speaker">KIM KITSURAGI</span> — "Detective, I highly advise against drinking from a thermos you found inside a rusted storm drain."
</p>

<!-- Skill check with result -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-skill electrochemistry">ELECTROCHEMISTRY</span>
  <span class="de-check success">[Medium: Success]</span> — But look at it! The strange, iridescent film resting on top of the liquid promises a kaleidoscope of illicit thrills. Just a little sip. It will make you feel like a god.
</p>

<!-- Skill check failure -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-skill volition">VOLITION</span>
  <span class="de-check failure">[Easy: Failure]</span> — The lieutenant is right. It's a blatant biohazard. But you are so, so weak against the call of the void.
</p>

<!-- Skill without a check (inner monologue / commentary) -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-skill half-light">HALF LIGHT</span> — FIRE. PURE ALKALINE FIRE IN YOUR VEINS. YOUR ORGANS ARE MELTING.
</p>

Player Choices

An ordered list with no visible numbers. Add class="selected" to the chosen option and class="old" to options the character has already picked in a previous scene.

  1. [Old choice] "Who do you think left this here?"
  2. [Selected choice] [Pick up the thermos and drink the mystery fluid.] "Bottoms up."
  3. [Normal choice] "You're right, Lieutenant. Let's step away from the puddle."
HTML 
<ol class="de-choices">
  <li class="old">[Old choice] "Who do you think left this here?"</li>
  <li class="selected">[Selected choice] [Pick up the thermos and drink the mystery fluid.] "Bottoms up."</li>
  <li>[Normal choice] "You're right, Lieutenant. Let's step away from the puddle."</li>
</ol>
