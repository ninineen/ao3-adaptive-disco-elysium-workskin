Copy this into your chapter body while the work skin is active. Switch to HTML mode in the AO3 editor before pasting.

Each element is shown in context. Mix and match — you don't have to use all of them.

Dialogue Lines

The de-convo class handles the hanging indent. The speaker or skill label goes in its own span at the start.

KIM KITSURAGI — "Detective, I highly advise against drinking from a thermos you found inside a rusted storm drain."

YOU — Add class="you" to give the player character a distinct colour.

THE STORM DRAIN — Add class="thing" for objects, signs, items, or any non-character label.

ELECTROCHEMISTRY [Medium: Success] — But look at it! The strange, iridescent film resting on top of the liquid promises a kaleidoscope of illicit thrills. Just a little sip. It will make you feel like a god.

VOLITION [Easy: Failure] — The lieutenant is right. It's a blatant biohazard. But you are so, so weak against the call of the void.

HALF LIGHT — FIRE. PURE ALKALINE FIRE IN YOUR VEINS. YOUR ORGANS ARE MELTING.

<!-- Named character speaking -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-speaker">KIM KITSURAGI</span> — "Detective, I highly advise against drinking from a thermos you found inside a rusted storm drain."
</p>

<!-- Player character (YOU) -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-speaker you">YOU</span> — "Your line here."
</p>

<!-- Object / item / sign -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-speaker thing">THE NOTICE BOARD</span> — Your text here.
</p>

<!-- Skill check with result -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-skill electrochemistry">ELECTROCHEMISTRY</span>
  <span class="de-check success">[Medium: Success]</span> — But look at it! The strange, iridescent film resting on top of the liquid promises a kaleidoscope of illicit thrills. Just a little sip. It will make you feel like a god.
</p>

<!-- Skill check failure -->
<p class="de-convo">
  <span class="de-skill volition">VOLITION</span>
  <span class="de-check failure">[Easy: Failure]</span> — The lieutenant is right. It's a blatant biohazard. But you are so, so weak against the call of the void.
</p>

<!-- Skill without a check (inner monologue / commentary) -->

  <span class="de-skill half-light">HALF LIGHT</span> — FIRE. PURE ALKALINE FIRE IN YOUR VEINS. YOUR ORGANS ARE MELTING.
</p>

Player Choices

An ordered list. Add class="selected" to the chosen option and class="old" to options already picked in a previous scene.

Skill checks get class="white-check" (parchment card). Add class="locked" on top for an unavailable check. High-stakes or dangerous checks use class="red-check".

  1. "Who do you think left this here?"
  2. "Bottoms up."
  3. "You're right, Lieutenant. Let's step away from the puddle."
  4. RHETORIC [Medium: Difficulty 10] "The thermos chose me, Kim."
  5. ELECTROCHEMISTRY [Godly: Difficulty 18 — LOCKED] Identify the compound by smell alone.
  6. HALF LIGHT [Legendary: Difficulty 16] Drink a second time. All in.
HTML 
<ol class="de-choices">
  <!-- Previously chosen -->
  <li class="old">"Who do you think left this here?"</li>

  <!-- Selected this scene -->
  <li class="selected">"Bottoms up."</li>

  <!-- Normal unchosen option -->
  <li>"You're right, Lieutenant. Let's step away from the puddle."</li>

  <!-- Skill check (parchment card) -->
  <li class="white-check">
    <span class="de-skill rhetoric">RHETORIC</span> [Medium: Difficulty 10] "The thermos chose me, Kim."
  </li>

  <!-- Locked skill check — add aria-disabled="true" for screen readers -->
  <li class="white-check locked" aria-disabled="true">
    <span class="de-skill electrochemistry">ELECTROCHEMISTRY</span> [Godly: Difficulty 18 — LOCKED] Identify the compound by smell alone.
  </li>

  <!-- Dangerous / red check -->
  <li class="red-check">
    <span class="de-skill half-light">HALF LIGHT</span> [Legendary: Difficulty 16] Drink a second time. All in.
  </li>
</ol>
